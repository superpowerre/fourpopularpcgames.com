About Assassin's Creed

Assassin's Creed is an action-adventure video game franchise created by Ubisoft. Set primarily during historical events and locations, the games follow a centuries-old struggle between the Assassins, who fight for peace with free will, and the Templars, who desire peace through control. Each game allows players to explore iconic periods, blending history with fiction.
